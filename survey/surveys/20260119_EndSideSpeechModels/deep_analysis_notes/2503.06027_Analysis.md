# Paper ID: Empowering Edge Intelligence: A Comprehensive Survey (arXiv:2503.06027)

## 1. 核心任务 (Task Definition)
- **现存问题**: The field of "On-Device AI" is moving fast, with fragmented literature on compression, hardware, and privacy.
- **论文解决**: A comprehensive survey defining "On-device AI models" and structuring the challenges/solutions.
- **意义**: Acts as the "Table of Contents" for our report, providing the necessary taxonomy.

## 2. 方法论归类 (Methodology Class)
- **核心类**: Literature Survey / Taxonomy.
- **核心分类学**:
    - **Optimization**: Quantization, Pruning, Knowledge Distillation.
    - **Hardware**: NPU, DSP, GPU, Heterogeneous Computing.
    - **Challenges**: Energy Efficiency, Thermal Constraints, Privacy, Real-time Latency.

## 3. 贡献总结 (Contribution)
- **Definition**: Clearly defines the scope of "On-device AI" (local processing, no cloud).
- **Future Trends**: Identifies "Foundation Models on Edge" and "Collaborative Edge-Cloud" as key trends.
