# Broad Overview: On-Device Speech Models (2025-2026)

## 1. Edge SLMs & Efficient Inference
*   **MobileLLM-Pro: On-Device LLM for Mobile** (arXiv 2511.06719) - Optimization of 1B-3B models for mobile.
*   **WhisperKit: On-device Real-time ASR with Billion-Scale Transformers** (arXiv 2507.10860) - High-performance ASR on device using CoreML/Metal optimization.
*   **Tiny-Align: Bridging ASR and LLM on the Edge** (arXiv 2411.13766, v2 Nov 2025) - Alignment of tiny ASR models with LLMs for edge.
*   **OpusLM: A Family of Open Unified Speech Language Models** (arXiv 2506.17611) - Unified speech-text models, potential for edge distillation.
*   **Gemma 3 on mobile and web with Google AI Edge** (Google Blog, Mar 2025) - Deployment of Gemma 3 (SLM) on mobile devices.
*   **Small language models in voice AI at the edge** (Gnani.ai, Nov 2025) - Industry perspective on SLMs for voice.
*   **Safeguarding Privacy in Edge Speech Understanding with Tiny Foundation Models** (arXiv 2502.01649) - Privacy-focused tiny models.

## 2. Multimodal & Generative Speech
*   **LLaMA-Omni2: LLM-based Real-time Spoken Chatbot with Autoregressive Streaming Speech Synthesis** (arXiv 2505.02625) - End-to-end spoken chatbot, low latency streaming.
*   **VocalNet: Speech LLM with Multi-Token Prediction for Faster and High-Quality Generation** (arXiv 2504.04060) - Acceleration of speech generation.
*   **Granite-speech: open-source speech-aware LLMs with strong English ASR capabilities** (arXiv 2505.08699) - IBM's open speech LLM.
*   **VoiceTextBlender: Augmenting LLMs with Speech Capabilities via Single-Stage Joint Fine-Tuning** (NAACL 2025) - Integration technique.
*   **WhisperFlow: speech foundation models in real time** (arXiv 2412.11272, v2 Apr 2025) - Streaming capabilities for foundation models.

## 3. Specialized Edge Tasks (KWS, Spatial, Interaction)
*   **ChipChat: Low-Latency Cascaded Conversational Agent in MLX** (arXiv 2509.00078) - Apple's MLX based efficient agent.
*   **LLAMAPIE: Proactive In-Ear Conversation Assistants** (arXiv 2505.04066) - Wearable specific speech agent.
*   **SING: Spatial Context in Large Language Model for Next-Gen Wearables** (arXiv 2504.08907) - Spatial audio + LLM on wearables.
*   **Flavors of Moonshine: Tiny Specialized ASR Models for Edge Devices** (arXiv 2509.02523) - Specialized tiny models.
*   **On-device Streaming Discrete Speech Units** (Interspeech 2025, arXiv 2506.01845) - Discrete units for efficient streaming.
*   **Device-Directed Speech Detection for Follow-up Conversations** (Apple ML Research) - Context awareness.

## 4. Surveys & Reviews
*   **Empowering Edge Intelligence: A Comprehensive Survey on On-Device AI Models** (arXiv 2503.06027) - General edge AI survey.
*   **When Large Language Models Meet Speech: A Survey on Integration Approaches** (arXiv 2502.19548) - Integration survey.
*   **Efficient Inference for Edge Large Language Models: A Survey** (SciOpen Dec 2025) - Inference optimization survey.

## 5. Multilingual & Global
*   **Omnilingual ASR: Open-Source Multilingual Speech Recognition for 1600+ Languages** (arXiv 2511.09690) - Massive multilingualism, potentially distillable.
